Our Services

Professional and reliable solar solutions tailored to your specific requirements.

Solar On-Grid System

Our Solar On-Grid systems are perfect for homeowners and businesses looking to reduce their electricity bills. These systems are connected directly to the public electricity grid. Any excess power your panels generate is fed back into the grid, often earning you credits from your utility company.

  • Significantly lower monthly electricity bills.
  • No need for expensive battery storage.
  • Seamless integration with the existing grid infrastructure.

Solar Off-Grid System

For locations where the grid is unreliable or unavailable, our Solar Off-Grid Systems provide complete energy independence. These systems store generated electricity in high-capacity battery banks, ensuring you have power 24/7, even during outages or at night.

  • 100% independent from utility companies.
  • Ideal for remote homes, cabins, and farms.
  • Includes intelligent hybrid inverters and deep-cycle battery storage.

Solar Water Pump (AC/DC)

Optimize your agricultural operations with our highly efficient solar water pumps. We offer both AC and DC pump solutions designed to provide a steady water supply for irrigation, livestock, and domestic use without the ongoing costs of diesel or grid electricity.

  • Zero fuel costs and minimal maintenance.
  • Environmentally friendly water extraction.
  • Durable components designed for harsh weather conditions.

Solar Fencing

Protect your valuable crops and property with our state-of-the-art solar fencing solutions. These fences deliver a safe, sharp shock to deter animals or intruders, functioning entirely off solar power. It's a low-cost, highly effective security measure for farms and estates.

  • Effective deterrence against wild animals and trespassers.
  • Operates autonomously without needing grid connection.
  • Safe for humans and animals (non-lethal shock).
