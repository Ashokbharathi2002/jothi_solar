What Happens After You Contact Us?

We understand that transitioning to solar energy is a significant decision. To ensure you have all the information you need, we follow a transparent and consultative process:

  • 1. Initial Discovery Call: Our solar advisors will reach out to discuss your current energy usage, typical monthly electricity bills, and your primary goals for going solar.
  • 2. Comprehensive Site Survey: Our expert engineers will visit your property to evaluate roof condition, space availability, shading issues, and structural integrity to design the most efficient system possible.
  • 3. Customized Proposal & ROI Analysis: You will receive a detailed, no-obligation quotation. This document will outline the exact equipment we recommend, the total upfront costs, projected energy generation, and a clear timeline for your return on investment.
  • 4. Seamless Installation & Handover: Once approved, our certified technicians handle everything—from securing necessary permits to the final grid connection and activation, ensuring a completely hassle-free experience for you.
